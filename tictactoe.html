How to Play

Two players take turns placing their mark (X or O) on a 3x3 grid. Get three in a row -- horizontally, vertically, or diagonally -- to win!

Player X always goes first. After each game, you can rematch or switch players.

Win Record

Every matchup is tracked. The fridge remembers who beats who! Check the Win Record to see head-to-head stats between family members.

Strategy Tips

Take the center if you can. If your opponent takes the center, grab a corner. Block your opponent's two-in-a-row before building your own.
