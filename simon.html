How to Play

Watch the colored buttons light up in a sequence. Then tap the buttons in the same order.

Each round adds one more step to the sequence. Keep going as long as you can remember!

The game ends when you tap the wrong button. Your score is the number of rounds you completed.

Daily Challenge

Everyone gets the same sequence each day. Compare your memory with your family!

Tips

Try grouping colors into patterns. Say the colors in your head as you watch. The sequence speeds up as you progress, so stay focused!
