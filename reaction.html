How to Play

Food emojis appear one at a time in the play area. Tap each one as fast as you can!

Every successful tap scores a point and a new snack appears instantly at a random position. You have 30 seconds to tap as many as possible.

Tips

Stay focused and keep your finger ready. Don't panic -- steady, accurate taps beat wild flailing. Watch for the bounce animation to know where the next target appears.
