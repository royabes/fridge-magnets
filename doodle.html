How to Play

  1. Pick who's drawing -- tap your avatar on the home screen
  2. See your word -- only the drawer peeks! Hide the screen from guessers
  3. Draw on the fridge -- use colors, sizes, and your finger
  4. Family shouts guesses -- everyone watching tries to guess the word
  5. Tap "Got It!" when someone guesses correctly
  6. Tap who guessed it -- both drawer and guesser earn points
  7. Pass the fridge -- next person draws!

Scoring

Drawer: +2 points when someone guesses correctly

Guesser: +3 points for guessing correctly

Skip/Timeout: No points for anyone

Tips

Start with big shapes, then add details. Use different colors to make things clearer. Don't write letters or numbers -- that's cheating!

You have 90 seconds per word. If it's too hard, skip it!
