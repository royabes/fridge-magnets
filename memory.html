How to Play

Tap a card to flip it over and reveal the food emoji underneath. Then tap another card to find its match.

If the two cards match, they stay face-up. If not, they flip back over. Find all 8 pairs to win!

Try to finish in as few moves as possible and as fast as you can.

Daily Challenge

Everyone gets the same card layout each day. Compare moves and time with your family!

Tips

Focus on remembering positions. Try to flip cards near ones you've already seen. Don't rush -- fewer moves beats faster time on the leaderboard.
