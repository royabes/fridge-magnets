How to Play

Answer 10 trivia questions from categories like Food, Animals, Science, Geography, and Fun Facts.

Tap one of the four answer buttons. If you're right, it flashes green. If you're wrong, your choice flashes red and the correct answer is shown in green.

No time limit per question -- take your time and think!
